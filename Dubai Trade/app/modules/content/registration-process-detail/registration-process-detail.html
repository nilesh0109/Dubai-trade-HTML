Dubai Customs Resistration Process

  • Dubai Customs is considered one of the leading government departments as it facilitates smooth movement of legitimate trade and leverages economic and social development by securing and protecting the integrity of Dubai's borders.

    Dubai Trade Portal renders all Dubai Customs e-services such as Mirsal 2 Customs Clearance, Acknowledgment Services, eclaim Inspection in addition to many more.

Pre-Requisites

  1. 1. License:

    Any company that holds a valid License in Dubai, any of the other Emirates, or GCC (Trading, Industrial, Professional, etc...) can register on Dubai Trade to get Dubai Customs business code (i.e. AE-0000001, OM-000001, SA-000001 etc...).

    Dubai companies can be registered as Local Trading or FZ Licensees based on the license issuing authority.

    All Other Emirates & GCC companies can register as Local Trading companies only.

  2. 2. Required Documents:
    • Passport copy of authorized person(for New Registration)
    • Undertaking letter for professional companies only
    • Proof of cargo handler facility (for Cargo Handlers)
