Certified Trade & Logistics Professional (CTLP)

The Certified Trade & Logistics Professional (CTLP) is a comprehensive and progressive vocational training programme that covers the end-to-end process of import and export in the UAE and the region. The hands-on programme is designed to give trainees in-depth understanding of the full business concepts and procedures related to all stakeholders involved in the trade supply chain
