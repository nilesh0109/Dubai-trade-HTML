7th ESEA Profile

Dubai Trade will be celebrating its 7th E-Services Excellence Award Ceremony under the patronage of HH Sheikh Maktoum Bin Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Deputy Ruler of Dubai. The event will be taking place on the 10th of February 2015 at the Samaya Ballroom, Ritz Carlton, DIFC. This year the award will be focused on the theme of Business Growth measured by the number of transactions throughout the year of 2014. ESEA is the first award in the region that is given based on actual adoption rates.

Award Criteria

This year, the winners are chosen based on a criteria that reflects the highest increase in business transactions with the following conditions:

  • Company should be registered with Dubai Trade Portal

  • Company should not be a winner for the past 3 years

  • Company’s Online adoption should be minimum 95% in 2014

  • Calculations based on online growth (transactions) between 2013 & 2014

  • The count of transactions will be of key e-Services

Dubai Trade will acknowledge nine winners that have achieved the highest growth in business transactions in the following trading communities:

  • Importer of the Year

  • Exporter of the Year

  • Re-Exporter of the Year

  • Shipping Agent of the Year – Containerized Cargo

  • Shipping Agent of the Year – General Cargo

  • Freight Forwarder of the Year

  • Clearing Agent of the Year

  • Haulier of the Year

  • Free Zone Company of the Year
