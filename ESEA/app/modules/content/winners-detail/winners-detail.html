The E-Services Excellence Award is an annual award ceremony organized by Dubai Trade in order to reward and recognize the best perform in e-Services adoption on the Dubai Trade portal. The ESEA features a new theme every year which targets a particular e-service with the main aim of encouraging the conversion to e-services, and award the highest performers.

In 2008, Dubai Trade announced its first ESEA collaboration with LOG.LEO, as it awarded the best performer in the e-Service available on Dubai Trade's portal known as e-Token.
